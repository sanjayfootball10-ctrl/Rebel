Introduction

More about football

Positions

  1. Forward-The who tries to score a goal.
  2. Midfield-This is important position because midfielders supports both defence and forward.
  3. Defence-In this are players stops opponent forward from scaring a goal
  4. Goalkeeper-He is tho one who stops the ball by using both hands(only within penalty field) and feet

Basic Rules

  • Game starts at the centre of the field.
  • A goal is scored when the ball fully crosses the goal line under the crossbar.
  • A player can't be nearer to the opponent's goal than the secon-last defender when receiving the ball.
  • Tackling from behind, holding, tripping or touching the ball with hands intentionally can lead to free kick or penalties.
  • Refree has two cards one is yellow which indicate warning and red which means sending off the player off the field.
